About Us

A hospital is an essential healthcare facility that provides patients in need with specialised medical care, nursing care, and necessary medical supplies. The most common kind is the general hospital, which is usually furnished with an emergency room that can handle any kind of medical emergency or accident.Hospitals are institutions that deal with health care activities. They offer treatment to patients with specialized staff and equipment. In other words, hospitals serve humanity and play a vital role in the social welfare of any society.The word "hospital" does not have a full form as it is not an acronym. It is derived from the Latin word "hospes," which means "guest" or "host." The term has evolved over time to refer to an institution providing medical and surgical treatment and nursing care for sick or injured people.One of the most significant advantages of choosing a hospital that offers all treatments under one roof is convenience.
